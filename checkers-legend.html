About Checkers Legend

A checkers game online is called Checkers Legend.
This strategic board game may be played either alone versus a computer with one of three difficulty levels,
or with a companion by taking turns at the keyboard.
The aim is to take control of every piece that the adversary has.

How to play Checkers Legend online?

Press W, D, X, Up, Right, or Mouse to move the cursor ahead.
Press S, A, Z or the Down or Left arrows to turn in the other direction.
