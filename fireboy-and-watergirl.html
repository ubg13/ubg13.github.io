About Fireboy & Watergirl Unblocked

FireBoy and WaterGirl
The boy and the girl arrive to the woodland temple in the first game in the well-known game series Fireboy and Watergirl.
To exit, Fireboy and Watergirl must go through many levels.
This two-player game can only be finished if the two players cooperate to solve each room's puzzle in the temple.
Both Fireboy and Watergirl are unable to walk into water or hot lava,
but they may still assist one another by pressing buttons and opening doors.

How to play Fireboy & Watergirl Unblocked online?

Press W, D, X, Up, Right, or Mouse to move the cursor ahead.
Press S, A, Z or the Down or Left arrows to turn in the other direction.
