About Color Tunnel Unblocked

Slope and Run 3 served as an inspiration for the quick-paced game called Tunnel Rush. Moving at breakneck speed through a colorful tunnel, you must dodge several obstacles of various kinds. To fast move your position away from the impending barrier and observe its location, you must be very nimble and accurate. Keep focused and beat your previous best!

How to play Color Tunnel Unblocked online?

Press W, D, X, Up, Right, or Mouse to move the cursor ahead.
Press S, A, Z or the Down or Left arrows to turn in the other direction.
