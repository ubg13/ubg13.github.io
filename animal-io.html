About Animal.io

Online multiplayer game called Animal.io features voxel art. On the deserted island, all of our critters are scurrying about and battling for food. Choose one of your animals and train it to bring in food and drive out any rival animals to become you the island's ruler.

How to play Animal.io online?

Press W, D, X, Up, Right, or Mouse to move the cursor ahead.
Press S, A, Z or the Down or Left arrows to turn in the other direction.
