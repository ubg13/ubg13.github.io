How do puzzle games work?
Puzzle mobile games are designed to test a variety of problem-solving abilities,
such as pattern identification, logic, word completion, and process comprehension.
Match-three puzzles, action puzzles, word games, trivia games, solitaire/mahjong games,
coloring books, and hidden object games are just a few of the sub-genres of puzzle games.
All different game genres may integrate puzzle game components.
How are puzzle games designed?
In order to go on to the next, more tougher challenge, players in puzzle games must first complete a logic problem or obstacle.
Depending on the sort of puzzle game, playing with several people may be required, although puzzle games are normally played alone.
